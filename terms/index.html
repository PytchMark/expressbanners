Terms

Clear terms for smooth production.

These guidelines help us deliver quality work on time.

Turnaround times

Standard turnaround ranges from 2-7 business days depending on service, volume, and finish. Rush options may be available by request and will be confirmed during approval.

Revisions & approvals

Digital proofs are provided for approval before production. Minor edits are included; larger changes may require additional time or fees. Approved proofs move directly into production.

File responsibility

Clients are responsible for providing accurate brand assets and copy. We review files for print readiness and will notify you if adjustments are needed.

Deposits & payments

Deposits may be required for larger orders. Card payments via NCB will be available soon; alternate payment methods are confirmed during order review.

Refunds & cancellations

Because custom print work is made to order, refunds are limited once production begins. If an error is made on our end, we will reprint or correct promptly.
